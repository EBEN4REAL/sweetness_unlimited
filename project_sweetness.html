PROJECT SWEETNESS

Hello!

Project sweetness, is all about using great stories to impact lives positively.

Storytelling is one of the most powerful tools in the world and a good story will not just entertain its target audience but also influence their attitudes towards life, yet not everyone can afford a good story (Art)

It is my utmost desire to create some special contents and distribute for free to some special people like: pupils, teenagers rural women and the physically challenged; but I can’t do it all by myself. For this to be achievable, I humbly request you consider making a modest financial support to this noble cause.

Your contribution will help our younger ones understand hard drug is an inevitable destroyer that only gives temporary escape from reality. It will help our teenage boys channel their masculinity into better productivity rather than growing into sexual predators, while our girls will grow up with proper understanding of dignity. It will help the rural women to know (among other things) that family planning enhances life and the physically challenged will get to know they are not second class citizens in our world.

Let’s join hands together to ooze sweetness across Nigeria and beyond.

To contribute offline, please call or text (+234) 7033349073 or email ourprojectsweetness@gmail.com .

Thank you.

Abigail Oridupa.
